Rewards Program

We take pride in customer loyalty, so we provide a very beneficial rewards program for our customers. Throughout the rewards program, after a certain number of purchases the customer will be gifted with some sort of reward, such as “50% off your next purchase” or “Buy one pair of shoes, get one free”. By doing this, we feel like this shows our appreciation for the customers that continue to come back and purchase our products.
