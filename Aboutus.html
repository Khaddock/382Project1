About Us

Michael and Michelle’s March Madness was founded in 1985 when a 17-year-old Michael Wu was playing at an AAU basketball tournament in Naperville, Illinois. During his warmup, he was approached by his teammate’s older sister, Michelle Jane, who suggested that Michael should get new shoes, since he was slipping when trying to do layups. Michael then learned that Michelle was a shoe designer and asked if she could design new shoes for him so he could better perform on the court. They began to work together and designed their first line of basketball shoes in December of 1985. From then on, they expanded their product line to a variety of high quality basketball equipment that ensured stability and support for athletes on the court. By the late 90’s, Michael and Michelle’s March Madness had twenty retail locations across the United States.

Today, headquartered in Chicago, IL, Michael and Michelle’s March Madness is a leading basketball goods retailer offering an extensive assortment of authentic, high-quality basketball equipment, apparel, footwear and accessories. The company serves and inspires athletes to achieve their personal best through a combination of its dedicated teammates, in-store services and unique specialty. In addition, Michael and Michelle’s March Madness offers its products through a dynamic eCommerce platform that is integrated with its store network and provides customers with the convenience and expertise of a 24-hour storefront.
